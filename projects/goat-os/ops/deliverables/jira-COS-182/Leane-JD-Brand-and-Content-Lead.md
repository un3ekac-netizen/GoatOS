# Job Description — Brand & Content Lead

**Company:** Hampr  
**Location:** Australia / New Zealand (Hybrid / On-site flexibility)  
**Employment Type:** Full-time  
**Reports To:** Chief of Staff / Head of Marketing  

---

## About Hampr

Hampr connects businesses with the best hospitality suppliers across Australia and New Zealand. We’re building the go-to platform for the hospitality industry — and we need a marketing all-rounder to help us tell that story.

---

## Role Purpose

The Brand & Content Lead is responsible for driving and executing Hampr’s marketing strategy to improve overall brand awareness and generate high-quality inbound leads. You will oversee the end-to-end marketing ecosystem, from high-level strategy and stakeholder collaboration to hands-on content production and performance reporting.

This role has evolved from pure content creation into a true ‘jack-of-all-trades’ marketing position. You’ll be the engine behind our brand voice, the architect of our content, and the glue that keeps our marketing operations running smoothly.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Online / Digital Marketing

**Website Marketing**
- Design and write compelling landing pages that convert
- Optimise site content for SEO and user experience

**Content Marketing**
- Produce and manage the LinkedIn Newsletter
- Design e-books, infographics, and downloadable resources
- Create photography and filmed content for campaigns
- Write and publish blog articles aligned with brand strategy

**Social Media Marketing**
- Manage and grow Hampr’s presence on LinkedIn and Instagram
- Plan, create, and schedule engaging content
- Monitor engagement and respond to community interactions

**Email Marketing**
- Build and execute EDM campaigns
- Segment audiences and optimise for open rates, click-throughs, and conversions

**Sales Enablement**
- Develop landing pages and collateral to support the sales team
- Ensure marketing materials align with sales objectives

---

### 2. Traditional / Offline Marketing

**Event Marketing**
- Plan and execute internal activation campaigns for active and new customers
- Coordinate external activation events to drive brand visibility
- Develop thought leadership content and support event speaker opportunities

---

### 3. Internal Admin / BAU

- Maintain the occasion calendar and update the ops portal with relevant occasions
- Fulfill internal team collateral requests
- Prepare and present marketing updates at townhall meetings

---

### 4. Brand Design & Management

- Own and enforce brand guidelines across all touchpoints
- Develop and maintain collateral templates
- Manage merchandise design and inventory

---

## Required Skills & Experience

- 3+ years experience in a marketing, content, or brand role (B2B or marketplace preferred)
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Proficiency in design tools (e.g., Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, Figma)
- Hands-on experience with social media management and email marketing platforms
- Comfortable with basic video filming and editing
- Strong organisational skills and ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
- Self-starter attitude — you figure things out and get them done

---

## Nice-to-Have Skills

- Experience in the hospitality, SaaS, or marketplace industries
- SEO and SEM knowledge
- Familiarity with marketing automation and CRM tools (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Photography or videography skills beyond basics
- Event management experience
- Data analysis and reporting capabilities

---

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports To:** Chief of Staff / Head of Marketing
- **Collaborates Closely With:** Sales, Operations, Product, and Leadership teams

---

## What Success Looks Like

- Consistent growth in brand awareness metrics across digital channels
- High-quality inbound lead generation tied to marketing campaigns
- A cohesive, recognisable brand presence across all customer touchpoints
- Smooth internal operations with timely delivery of collateral and reporting
